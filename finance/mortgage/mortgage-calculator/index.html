How to Use This Mortgage Calculator

To calculate your estimated mortgage payment:

  1. Enter the home price
  2. Adjust the down payment amount or percentage
  3. Select your preferred loan term
  4. Enter the interest rate
  5. Add property tax, home insurance, and HOA fees if applicable
  6. Click "Calculate" to see your results

Understanding Your Results

The calculator provides a comprehensive breakdown of your mortgage costs:

  • Monthly Payment Breakdown: Shows how your monthly payment is divided between principal, interest, taxes, insurance, and other costs.
  • Loan Summary: Provides an overview of your loan amount, total interest paid over the life of the loan, and the total cost of the loan.
  • Amortization Schedule: Displays how your loan balance decreases over time as you make payments, broken down by year.

Mortgage Payment Components

Your mortgage payment typically consists of several components:

  • Principal: The amount you borrowed and need to pay back.
  • Interest: The cost of borrowing money, calculated as a percentage of your loan balance.
  • Property Taxes: Annual taxes assessed by your local government based on your property's value.
  • Homeowners Insurance: Protects your home against damage, theft, and liability.
  • Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI): Required if your down payment is less than 20% of the home's value.
  • HOA Fees: Monthly fees paid to a homeowners association for maintenance of common areas and amenities.
